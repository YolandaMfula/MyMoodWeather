About MoodWeather

MoodWeather helps you log your daily moods and see how the weather may influence how you feel.

It combines real-time weather data with a simple mood tracker and chart visualization.

This app was built as a fun way to explore how external environments like weather may correlate with your emotions.

Features:

  • Real-time weather data for any city
  • Simple mood logging with emoji interface
  • Visual mood trends with charts
  • User authentication and profiles
  • Dark/light theme support
